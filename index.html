Listening practice

Get reps on staying with someone.

A rehearsal space for peer-support listening. A fictional person opens up; you write how you'd respond; you get feedback on whether you stayed non-directive — companioning, not fixing.

This is practice, not a counseling line. It's a skills rehearsal for people already in supervised peer-support training. It doesn't certify anyone, replace your training or supervisor, or handle anyone in real distress. If a real person needs support, follow your program's actual protocol.
Aim to reflect, validate, and open — not to solve.
Built as a practice companion, not a clinical tool. Scenarios are fictional. Your responses are sent to the model only to generate feedback and aren't stored by this page.
